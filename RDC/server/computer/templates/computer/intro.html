Welcome to the Westholme Computer Management System

This website allows you to manage and keep track of various computers. It provides information about each computer, including its name, model, AnyDesk connection details, and associated user.

You can view the list of computers, search for specific computers, and perform other administrative actions depending on your privileges.

Feel free to navigate through the website and explore the computer management features. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact the system administrator.
